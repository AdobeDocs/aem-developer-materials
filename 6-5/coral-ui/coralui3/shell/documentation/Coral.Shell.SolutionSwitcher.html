Coral.Shell.SolutionSwitcher

Current Version: 2.0.2
Relevant Specifications: Solution Switcher
Github: @coralui/coralui-component-shell-solutionswitcher

The Shell.SolutionSwitcher component provides an easy way to quickly switch between solutions and core services. The intended UI pattern is to have this panel slide out from underneath item is activated. Providing quick access to other Adobe solutions establishes a clear connection between the current context and other Adobe offerings, both active or inactive.

In implementation, it is important to include inactive solutions in large and small viewports, with intelligent paths to account linking where applicable.
