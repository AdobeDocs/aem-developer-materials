Backdrop Example

|

The backdrop won't be visible until you add the is-open class.

Due to the way the browser works, you'll have to wait an animation frame after showing the backdrop before adding the is-open class. You can use Coral.commons.nextFrame to do tihs.

Additionally, you'll have to wait 350ms after removing the is-open class beforing hiding the the overlay so the animation can complete. You can use Coral.mixin.overlay.FADETIME instead of hardcoding the time here.
