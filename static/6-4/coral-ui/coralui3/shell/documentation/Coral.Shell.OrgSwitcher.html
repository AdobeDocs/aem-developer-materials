Coral.Shell.OrgSwitcher

Current Version: 1.4.2
Github: @coralui/coralui-component-shell-orgswitcher

The Shell3 Organization Switcher component can fill a Shell.Menu.Item with an interface to let a user change what organizational area of their application they are using. In the simplest sense it is just a drop down menu with a search feature.
