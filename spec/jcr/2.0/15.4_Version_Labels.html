15.4 Version Labels

A version label is a JCR name (see ยง3.2 Names) associated with a version. A version may have zero or more labels. Within a given version history, a particular label may appear a maximum of once. Labels are typically used to add application-level information to a stored version.

Under simple versioning labels are added, accessed and removed only through the version-label-specific API.
