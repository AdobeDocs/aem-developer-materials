15.7.7 Simple vs. Full Versioning after Restore

Under simple versioning N is automatically checked-in.

Under full versioning the jcr:isCheckedOut property of N is set to false (though the other elements of a check-in are not performed). Additionally, the jcr:baseVersion property of N is set to V. Note that after the next check-out (see ยง15.3 Check-Out) and subsequent check-in of N the version V will acquire an additional direct successor, forming a branch.
